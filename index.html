Changes Applied
Romanization rules: Word-final aa shortens to a (एउटा → eut'a). Word-final inherent a is dropped (देस → des). Nipat / postpositions keep their vowels and are space-separated (मा → maa, को → ko, ले → le, र → ra). Established international proper nouns keep conventional English spelling (Nepal, Kathmandu). Sentences begin with a capital letter.
