Reformed Nepali Alphabet

Phonetically rationalized orthography — Krish Simkhada · 2026

Simplification Principles

Every change removes a distinction that no longer exists in spoken Nepali. Nothing is merged that speakers still distinguish.

ChangeTraditionalReformedReason
Vowel length mergedई → इ   ऊ → उइ   उह्रस्व/दीर्घ distinction not maintained in speech
Sibilants unifiedश / ष → ससAll three pronounced identically
Retroflex nasal mergedण → ननIndistinguishable from dental nasal in speech
Labial approximant mergedव → बबMerges with ब in natural speech
Conjunct reformedज्ञ → ग्यग्यNepali pronunciation is gya, not Sanskrit jña
Conjunct reformedक्ष → क्सक्सष merges into स; conjunct follows
ङ, ञ limitedङ   ञङ   ञRetained as conjunct nasals only (अङ्क, पञ्च)

Sample Paragraph — Reformed Devanagari

नेपाल एउटा सुन्दर देस हो। यहाँ अग्लो हिमाल, हरिया जंगल र फराकिला उपत्यकाहरु छन्। नेपालिहरु आफ्नो संस्क्रिति र परम्परामा बिस्बास गर्छन्। यहाँका मानिसहरु साहसि र परिस्रमि छन्। हाम्रो देसको प्राकृतिक सौन्दर्यले संसारभरका पर्यटकहरुलाइ आकर्सित गर्छ।

Same Paragraph — Romanized

Nepal eut'a sundar des ho. Yahaan aglo himaal, hariya jangal ra pharaakila upatyaakaharu chhan. Nepaliharu aaphno sanskriti ra parampara maa bisbaas garchhan. Yahaan ka maanisaharu saahasi ra parisrami chhan. Haamro des ko praakritik saundarya le sansaarbhar ka paryat'akaharu laai aakarsit garchha.
